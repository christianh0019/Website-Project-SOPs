# SOP: Converting the Resources Hub

**ID:** SOP-18
**Type:** Hub Page
**Reference Implementation:** `src/pages/Resources.tsx`

## 1. The Strategy: "The Traffic Director"
This page catches users who are looking for information but aren't sure where to go (or clicked "Resources" in the nav).
**Core Objective**: Route traffic efficienty to the 6 pillars of the brand.

## 2. Structural Blueprint
-   **Header**: Standard Page Header.
-   **The Grid**:
    -   Layout: 3 Columns.
    -   Content: Cards for Process, About, Blog, Partners, Careers, Portfolio.
-   **Visuals**:
    -   Icon-heavy (Clean, professional look).
    -   Short descriptions (1 sentence).

## 3. The Pillars (Mandatory Links)
1.  **Our Process**: "How we build."
2.  **About Us**: "Who we are."
3.  **Blog**: "Insights."
4.  **Strategic Partners**: "For Realtors/Trades."
5.  **Careers**: "Join the team."
6.  **Portfolio**: "Our work."

## Checklist
- [ ] All 6 pillars are present.
-   [ ] Icons are consistent (Lucide React or similar).
-   [ ] Links work and point to correct internal paths.
